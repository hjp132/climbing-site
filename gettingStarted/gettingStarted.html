Getting Started

1 - Get on the Wall

Check out local climbing gyms in your area, they will be more than happy to see new faces and help out with your first time climbing. Chances are that there is a climbing gym locally to you. This is a great place to start as there will most likely be some form of grading system in place to show you the difficulty, each different 'route' or 'problem' should be graded so make sure to try out a few easier ones.

Typically indoor climbing is divided into three disciplines. Bouldering, top roping, and lead climbing. Each of these vary in technique, but the objective are all the same: get to the top.
