IFSC 2020 - Moscow:
Over 21st November - 29th November 2020, the IFSC Europe Championships happend. This was following the expected ruleset for the Tokyo 2020 climbing rules which meant every climber had to participate in every discipline (Speed, lead, bouldering) which meant that you would generally see climbers excel at one rather than the other meaning you would see varied leaderboards.

For a lot of the competitors countries will use this to benchmark to see who will be going to the Olympics next year.
