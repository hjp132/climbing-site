Bouldering

Bouldering is a sport that generally takes place indoors. This is a sport that I would say is the most entry level of all of them as it is much lower to the ground and is a lot safer. With Bouldering you can learn the basic mechanics of climbing. Bouldering is one of the disciplines that will be featuring in the 2021 olympics.

Bouldering is a great alternative to other disciplines because you can do it casually and with little preperation. If you live in or near a town or city there is a high likelyhood there is a bouldering place near you.
