Free Solo

Free Solo Climbing is most known for how dangerous of a discipline this is of climbing. Free Soloing is done without the aid of any supporting or protective gear at all. At most the climber would have a chalk bag with them, that would be it. Free Soloing is probably most known by Alex Honnold who famously climbed the 2,900 foot Freerider route of El Capitan. Completing the climb in 3 hours and 56 minutes.

Free Soloing is not a sport that is casually done and normally takes weeks / months worth of preperation before someone attempts to climb something of this level. Free Soloing is something that someone should do after they have been climbing for a long time, you shouldn't be trying this casually.
