Mountaineering

Mountaineering / Alpinism is the sport of climbing a mountain, the term is also used sometimes for walking up low mountains. People of the sport generally stick to the same climate of mountain; colder, icey climates being more dangerous. Due to climate change this climate is becoming more and more dangerous. For the untrained, Mountaineering is dangerous pastime.

Comparing Mountaineering to other climbing, this one is similar to trad climbing & free soloing due to the danger element to the sport.
