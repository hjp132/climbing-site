Sport Climbing

Sport Climbing is the act of climbing a single or multi pitched routes, that are protected and secured by permanently-fixed bolts and anchors drilled into the rock, using a rope and the aid of a belayer ( a person at the bottom who stops you from falling all the way down).

The Main difference between sport climbing and bouldering is the height of the routes being climbed and the form of protection ( bouldering = no ropes, with crash pads). Where as, traditional climbing means you will have to install those bolts and anchors yourself.
