Getting Started
In this blog I will go through how to get started!

1

Find a climbing gym near you
Use Google Maps, or something to find a local climbing gym. Probably better to start at a bouldering gym.

2

Go climbing, learn the basics
Practice makes perfect, learn the basic techniques and climbs to get started.

3

Look into Climbing Disciplines
You can find our guide on climbing genres here, alternatively do some resarch into them!

4

Find a mentor or watch people so you can learn
You can find a huge variety of people to learn from, anywhere from local people to people online.

5

Have fun
Make sure you have fun
